Red Panda

They are wild Red Pandas only live in Asia. Winter is the red pandas favourite time of the year. The giant pandas and red pandas both have something in common. Red Pandas like to stay in mountainous mixed deciduous and conifer forests.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus Fulgens
  • Lifespan: 14 years or longer
  • Family: Ailuridae
  • Habitat: Eastern Himalayas

Red Pandas are wild animals with round heads, large in size, short snouts and pointed ears. Their faces are white with reddish-brown "tear" that is from the eyes from the corner of the mouth. These marks help them to keep the sun out of their eyes. Their tailis buff rings. The red pandas are skilled climbers and using trees for shelter.
